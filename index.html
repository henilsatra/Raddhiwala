About Us

Raddhiwala.com © is a subsidary of a forty year old legacy - Mahavir Paper Mart. We are a family-owned and operated scrap and waste paper recycling business that has been serving the community for over 40 years. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service and helping to create a cleaner and healthier planet.

We are a licensed and insured scrap and waste paper recycling company, and we are committed to following all environmental regulations. We also offer competitive prices and prompt pickup and delivery. We are proud to be a part of our community, and we are committed to making a positive impact on the environment.

We believe that recycling scrap and waste paper is a great way to reduce waste, conserve resources, and protect the environment. If you have any scrap and waste paper that you would like to recycle, please contact us today. We would be happy to help you recycle your scrap and waste paper and make a difference for the environment.



Our Mission

To provide our customers with a convenient, reliable, and environmentally friendly way to recycle their scrap metal.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable scrap management solutions by continuing to innovate and develop new ways to recycle scrap materials, and by working with our partners to create a more sustainable future.
